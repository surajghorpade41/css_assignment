ABOUT US

It all started at the INDIAN TECHNOLOGY CONGRESS on Sept 14-15th 2019, NIMHANS convention centre,Bangalore. The UNISEC INDIA are tied up with our satellite club.The club was born on feb 19th, 2020 and was named ANTARIKSH,KSIT SATELLITE CLUB.The highly enthusiastic HODs are the main reason behind the formation of the club. The faculty co-ordinators were appointed and the student office bearers were also.On the occasion of the inagural function of the club various events were organised and were carried out very well with the immense support of the staff and the student co-ordinators.The ANTARIKSH was inagurated by Dr.Goplakrishnan who is the founder of UNISEC INDIA.His presence on the inagural day gave a major boost to the function. Later on, the club went on well and had organised a technical talk on the cansats and cubsats. Having followed by the webinar on SYSTEM ENGINEERING by Padmashri Prof.R.M.Vasagam, who was the man behind the first geostationary satellite of India,APPLE.
